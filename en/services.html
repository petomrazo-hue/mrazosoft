Service questions

Frequently asked questions

How long does a website or e-shop take?

A smaller website is usually ready in 1–3 weeks; e-shops and apps depend on scope. We agree the deadline up front.

Do you work on existing websites, PrestaShop or WordPress?

Yes. I handle changes, speed fixes, new features, modules, integrations and migrations on existing sites and e-shops.

Can you build an app for Android and iPhone?

Most often I build a PWA that runs on Android, iPhone and desktop from a single code base. For specific needs I recommend the right approach.

What does automation & AI include?

Python tools, scraping, report generation, system integrations and practical AI features that save repetitive manual work.

What does the website audit include?

I measure speed (Google Lighthouse) and check SEO, security, cookies and GDPR essentials. You get a plain-language report with fixes ordered by priority — usable with any supplier.

Can the website grow later?

Yes. I build sites so new pages and features can be added after launch.

How much does a website or e-shop cost?

Price always depends on scope — I will gladly walk you through it when you get in touch. More in the article How much does a website cost (Slovak).
